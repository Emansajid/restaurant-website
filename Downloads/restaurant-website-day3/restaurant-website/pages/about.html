Who We Are

A Passion for Food,
A Love for People

Saveur was born from a simple belief: that great food has the power to bring people together, create memories, and transcend the ordinary. Nestled in the heart of Lahore, we've been serving exceptional cuisine for over 15 years, welcoming guests from all walks of life.

Our kitchen is a melting pot of global culinary traditions, guided by award-winning chefs who source only the finest seasonal ingredients. From our wood-fired pizzas to our perfectly seared steaks, every dish is a reflection of our relentless pursuit of excellence.

But Saveur is more than the food on your plate — it's the warmth of our service, the elegance of our space, and the joy on your face as you take that first perfect bite.

Our Journey

The Story Behind Saveur

From a humble dream to one of Lahore's most celebrated dining destinations — here's how it all began.

2008 · The Beginning

Opening Our Doors

Chef Amir Khan opened Saveur with just 12 tables and an unwavering vision — to serve honest, exceptional food in an elegant setting. The first week was fully booked.

08
12

2012 · Recognition

First Major Award

Saveur won the Lahore Fine Dining Excellence Award, putting us on the national culinary map. Our BBQ menu was featured in three national food magazines.

2016 · Expansion

Growing the Family

We expanded to a larger venue with a dedicated events hall, wood-fired pizza oven, and a full dessert bar. Our team grew to 45 passionate professionals.

16
20

2020 · Resilience

Adapting & Innovating

During challenging times, we launched our signature meal kits and online ordering platform, reaching 10,000+ home kitchens across Pakistan.

2024 · Today

A New Chapter

Now serving 50,000+ satisfied guests annually, we continue to innovate our menu seasonally while staying true to the values that started it all — passion, quality, and heart.
